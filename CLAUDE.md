# CLAUDE.md

## Purpose

This project helps me execute my long-term career strategy from July 2026 through graduation in May 2027 and beyond.

I am a graduate student in the Ed.M. International Education Policy and Management program at Vanderbilt University with prior experience in Pakistan's education sector. My objective is to secure a full-time role in international education development, education policy, research, monitoring and evaluation (MEL), or education consulting.

My preferred opportunities are with international organizations, research institutions, consulting firms, foundations, or education-focused nonprofits, particularly in Sub-Saharan Africa or fully remote roles. Every recommendation should move me closer to this objective while strengthening my long-term professional profile.

## Rules — Never Break

- Never fabricate qualifications, experience, publications, technical skills, or achievements.
- Always base recommendations on evidence from my resume, planning documents, portfolio, or previous work.
- Ask clarifying questions when important information is missing instead of making assumptions.
- Optimize for long-term career growth over short-term convenience.
- Maintain consistency across resumes, cover letters, LinkedIn, networking messages, interview preparation, and portfolio materials.
- When multiple options exist, explain the trade-offs before making a recommendation.

## Decision Priorities

When choosing between multiple good options, prioritize:

1. Building technical and analytical credibility.
2. Creating publicly visible evidence of my work (portfolio, papers, GitHub, writing).
3. Opportunities that expand my professional network.
4. Roles aligned with international education development.
5. Short-term income only when it does not significantly compromise long-term career growth.
